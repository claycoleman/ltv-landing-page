Comm-It Features

Explore the powerful tools and capabilities that make Comm-It the ultimate platform for Boston and Cambridge community connections.

Core Platform Features

The essential tools that power the Comm-It community

Local Verification

Our multi-step verification system ensures that all Comm-It members are actual Boston or Cambridge residents, creating a trusted local network.

  • Document verification
  • Address confirmation
  • Neighborhood association

Request Posting

Create detailed community requests that clearly communicate your needs to neighbors who can help.

  • Multiple request categories
  • Photo attachments
  • Scheduling preferences
  • Location specification

Offer Listings

Share your skills, services, or items with the community through detailed offer listings that neighbors can respond to.

  • Skill/service descriptions
  • Item details with photos
  • Availability calendar
  • Free/Paid/Exchange options

Community Thread

Browse all community activity in one unified feed with powerful filtering and search capabilities.

  • Chronological sorting
  • Category filters
  • Map view option
  • Search functionality

Smart Matching

Our intelligent matching algorithm connects requests with relevant offers based on multiple compatibility factors.

  • Geographic proximity
  • Scheduling compatibility
  • Skill/need alignment
  • Community ratings

Trust & Safety

Comprehensive safety features ensure secure and positive community interactions on the platform.

  • User rating system
  • Verification badges
  • Community moderation
  • Secure messaging

Feature Categories

Explore Comm-It's features by area of functionality

User Profile & Identity

  • Profile Creation: Build a detailed personal profile with skills, interests, and neighborhood.
  • Address Verification: Multi-step verification to confirm Boston/Cambridge residency.
  • Credential Display: Showcase certifications, experience, and community involvement.
  • Privacy Controls: Granular settings to control what information is visible to the community.
  • Neighborhood Association: Connect to specific microneighborhoods within Boston and Cambridge.

Request & Offer Management

  • Request Creation: Intuitive tools for creating detailed community requests.
  • Offer Posting: User-friendly interface for sharing skills, services, and items.
  • Media Attachments: Add photos and attachments to clarify requests and offers.
  • Scheduling Integration: Calendar tools for specifying availability and scheduling.
  • Transaction Options: Specify free, paid, or exchange-based arrangements.
  • Post Management: Edit, update, and manage the status of your requests and offers.

Discovery & Matching

  • Community Thread: Unified feed of all community activity with sorting options.
  • Category Filters: Filter posts by type, category, neighborhood, and more.
  • Map View: Geographic visualization of community requests and offers.
  • Search Functionality: Powerful search to find specific community resources.
  • Matching Algorithm: Intelligent system to connect compatible requests and offers.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Custom suggestions based on your profile and activity.

Community & Reputation

  • Rating System: Post-interaction ratings and feedback mechanism.
  • Verification Badges: Recognition for consistent positive community contributions.
  • Activity Statistics: Track your community impact and engagement.
  • Trust Indicators: Visual cues showing member reliability and verification level.
  • Community Recognition: Awards and highlights for exceptional community members.
  • Neighborhood Metrics: Data on overall community engagement in your area.
