Our Story

How Comm-It came to be

Comm-It was born from a simple observation: despite living in vibrant, densely populated urban neighborhoods, many Boston and Cambridge residents felt disconnected from their immediate communities. In our fast-paced world, we often don't know the talents, resources, and needs that exist just steps from our doorsteps.

Our founders, all long-time residents of the Boston area, experienced this disconnect firsthand. When co-founder Sarah needed help moving a couch, she ended up hiring an expensive service, only to later discover that her neighbor two floors down would have been happy to help. Similarly, when David had extra produce from his community garden plot, he had no easy way to share it with nearby neighbors who might appreciate fresh vegetables.

These experiences sparked conversations about how technology could bridge these community gaps, leading to the creation of Comm-It in 2022. What began as a simple community board concept has evolved into a comprehensive platform designed specifically for the unique needs of Boston and Cambridge communities.

Our Values

The principles that guide Comm-It

Community First

We believe in the power of local communities. Every feature and decision we make puts the needs of Boston and Cambridge residents at the forefront, prioritizing connections that strengthen neighborhoods.

Trust & Safety

We're committed to creating a platform where neighbors can interact with confidence. Our verification processes, community guidelines, and safety features are designed to build and maintain trust.

Inclusivity

We strive to make Comm-It accessible and welcoming to all Boston and Cambridge residents, regardless of background, income level, or technical expertise. Our platform is designed to bridge divides, not create them.

Resource Optimization

We believe in making the most of existing community resources. By facilitating sharing and exchange, we help reduce waste and environmental impact while maximizing the value of what's already in our neighborhoods.

Local Empowerment

We're dedicated to giving community members the tools they need to help themselves and each other. By enabling direct connections, we empower neighbors to create solutions that work for their specific needs.

Continuous Improvement

We're committed to constantly evolving the platform based on community feedback and needs. Comm-It is built by and for the community, and we believe in transparent, responsive development.
